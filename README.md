# Prompter — Free Teleprompter

**Read your script while recording video. No ads, no sign-up, no payment.**

[**Open Prompter**](https://leanri.github.io/prompter/)

---

## What is it?

Prompter is a free teleprompter app that scrolls your text on screen while you record video with your camera. Perfect for:

- YouTube videos & content creation
- Presentations & speeches
- Social media recordings (TikTok, Instagram Reels, etc.)
- Podcasts & voiceovers
- Any time you need to read while looking at the camera

## Features

- **Scrolling text over camera** — read your script while recording
- **Adjustable speed** — slow to fast, change anytime even during recording
- **Draggable reading line** — move the guide line to match your eye level
- **Front & back camera** — switch with one tap
- **HD video recording** — 720p, 1080p Full HD, or 4K
- **Save to Photos** — on iPhone, saves directly to your photo album
- **Any language** — English, Russian, Spanish, Chinese — just type or paste
- **Import text** — paste from ChatGPT, Notes, or import a .txt file
- **Save scripts** — save and reuse your scripts locally
- **Mirror mode** — flip camera for external teleprompter setups
- **Countdown timer** — 3-second countdown before recording starts
- **Works offline** — no internet needed after first load
- **Install as app** — add to your home screen, works like a native app

## How to use

1. Open [**leanri.github.io/prompter**](https://leanri.github.io/prompter/) on your phone or computer
2. Type or paste your script
3. Adjust font size, speed, and video quality
4. Tap **Start Prompter**
5. Allow camera access
6. Drag the blue line to your eye level
7. Tap the red record button when ready
8. Read your text and look at the camera
9. Stop recording — video saves to your device

## Install on iPhone

1. Open the link in Safari
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with arrow)
3. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Done — Prompter appears as an app on your home screen

## Install on Android

1. Open the link in Chrome
2. Tap **"Install"** when the banner appears (or tap menu → "Install app")
3. Done — Prompter appears in your app drawer

## Privacy

- **No account needed** — just open and use
- **No data collected** — nothing is sent to any server
- **Scripts stay on your device** — stored locally in your browser
- **Camera & mic access** — used only for recording, never uploaded anywhere

Built as a Progressive Web App (PWA) — a single HTML file with no dependencies. Works on any modern browser.
